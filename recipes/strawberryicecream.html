Strawberry Ice Cream

What's there to make you full after a any meal? That's right! It's Ice cream

Make your hot summer disappear with this refreshing taste of cold and frozen strawberries which you can easily make at home With just a couple of Ingredients you are sure to impress your friends and family

Ingredients

  • Strawberries:This homemade ice cream starts with two cups of mashed fresh strawberries.
  • Milk and Cream: Whole milk and heavy cream are essential to this rich homemade strawberry ice cream.
  • Sugar:You'll need a cup of white sugar for this sweet, fruity dessert.
  • VanillaandSalt: Vanilla extract and salt enhance the overall flavor of the strawberry ice cream.
  • Food Coloring: While red food coloring isnâ€™t required, it helps achieve the vibrant color you normally associate with strawberry ice cream.

Steps

  1. Combine the Ingredients in a large bowl
  2. Transfer the mixture to the freezer bowl of an ice cream maker
  3. Freeze according to the manufacturer's directions
  4. Transfer the ice cream to an airtight container and freeze until firm

Storing Procedures

Store your strawberry ice cream in an airtight storage container, recycled ice cream container, or zip-top freezer bag. Try to enjoy the ice cream within a few weeks for the best taste.
